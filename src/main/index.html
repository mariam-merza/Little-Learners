Welcome to Little Learners Home Daycare!

About Me

Hi, my name is Reem Maqadsy. I am a licensed home daycare owner located in Whitby, Ontario. I have 15 years of experience working with children and running a daycare from home. I am fully licensed with Bright Minds and first-aid certified. In a world that is rapidly changing everyday, I aim to create a safe and enjoyable space for children to learn and grow. I do educational games, creative play, and various arts and crafts. My backyard is fully fenced and we go outside to play on a daily basis, or depending on the weather. I provide healthy, homemade meals and snacks. My daycare is a home away from home for your little one.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How much do you charge?
    My rate is $25 per day. I offer a sibling discount of $20 per child. Before and after school care is $30
  2. Where are you located?
    I am located near Dryden and Garden. My home school is Fallingbrook Public School.
    The Julie Payette bus for French Immersion picks up in front of my house.
    Ormiston Public School and St. Matthew Catholic School are both within walking range from my house.
  3. How many children do you care for?
    There are currently 4 children under my care.
  4. Do you have space for my child? If not, is there a waiting list and how long is it?
    As of now, I have 3 open positions as listed above in my services. Should these positions be taken, you can send me a message through my contact section. There will be a waitlist displayed under the header there.
  5. How flexible are pick-up and drop-off times?
    Drop-offs start as early as 5:30am EDT. The latest time for pick-ups is 5:00pm EDT.
  6. Can you accomodate our family's dietary needs?
    I would be pleased to. Simply let me know and I will create a specific meal plan for your child.
  7. What will my child do throughout the day?
    I have a comprehensive meal and activity schedule that I update weekly. Activities include playing in the backyard, having a dance party, reading and writing, and arts and crafts. There are also daily naps scheduled in the afternoon.
